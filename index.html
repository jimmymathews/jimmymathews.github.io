I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center. Previously, I was a PhD student in the mathematics department at Stony Brook University, until the summer of 2017. I am also an anarchist, a luddite, a communist, and a utopian. I believe in the future promise of technological instrumentalism and democratic engineering to end poverty, end disenfranchisement, and promote free scholarship.
