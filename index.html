I am a researcher in the Nadeem Lab at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center. I was previously a scientific data engineer at the Allen Institute, a postdoctoral researcher in the Deasy Lab, and before that a PhD student in the mathematics department at Stony Brook University.

My research interests include:

  • Systems biology and complex systems. Graphs, categories, and operads.
  • Software design patterns and database design patterns. OWL ontology.
  • Philosophy of biology and science.
  • Low-dimensional geometry. Synthetic projective and differential geometry.
  • Differential forms and geometric PDEs.
  • Lie groups and their representations. Tensor and symmetry methods in data analysis.
